Welcome to the parimutuel market simulator

This tool simulates a 42.space-style dynamic parimutuel market over an 8×8 grid of soccer scorelines (Manchester City vs. Crystal Palace, goals 0..6 and ≥7). Agents mint outcome tokens against a bonding curve p(x) = x3/4; one cell is drawn as the winner and the entire pool is paid pro-rata to holders of that outcome.

  • 1
    Pick a preset on the left, or tweak individual parameters. Hover the ? icons to learn what each one does.
  • 2
    Run the simulation — Quick takes a few seconds, Detailed can take a minute or two.
  • 3
    Read the results: high-level KPIs at the top, then heatmaps over the outcome grid, then per-agent economics, then a per-trial drill-down.
Tip: try toggling the Agent strategy from random to weighted by marginal payout and watch how the supply distribution and agent ROI change.
